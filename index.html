We don't just teach STEM, we foster & build a community.

We focus on exposure and empowerment - our sessions have the goal of introducing a variety of STEM concepts and making science exciting while at the same time showing the students that they can be successful in STEM through the STEM professional guest aspect of our sessions.

Our Mission

Show kids that they do not have to become a product of their environment.

See our extended message and CONCAUSA application here

STEM for the Streets is a student-led non-profit organization aimed at exposing historically disenfranchised and under-served children to STEM through hands-on science experiments and engaging lesson plans accompanied by scientists, doctors, and engineers. Founded on the belief that every child deserves an equal opportunity, STEM for the Streets shows kids that the world is bigger than their neighborhood and that they don't have to become a product of their environment.

Aditya Gude

Aditya Gude is a board of directer for STEM for the Streets, a leader, an entrepreneur, and a social activist. As a incoming freshman at the University of Virginia, he is pursuing a career in the medical field to become a pediatric surgeon. Although born in America, he has not forgotten his roots and found inspiration from the high poverty prevalent in Vijayawada, India and fervently strives to give equal opportunity to those less fortunate. He has changed countless lives as a HIV Tester and Counselor working in clinics throughout the DC metropolitan area such as in the Latin American Youth Center and DC Health and Wellness Clinic.
