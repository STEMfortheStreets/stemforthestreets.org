Our Programs

Our Mission Ensuring Equal Opportunity within STEM

STEM for the Streets is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to familiarize BIPOC youth to STEM concepts with interactive labs and experiments while, at the same time, creating a community to foster academic success.

Mission

About Us Fostering a Community Around an Interest in STEM

STEM for the Streets aims to introduce various concepts in science, engineering, and technology to underpriveleged, BIPOC youth.

Our Methodology

​SFTS believes that the path to success lies in the community, which is why we emphasize learning as a group so much during our sessions.

BIPOC STEM Profressionals

For our labs, we invite BIPOC engineers, doctors, and scientists to help explain and mentor students so they can gain a more enriching experience.

Hands-On Experiments

Learning-by-doing gives the students an intuitive understanding of the material in every lesson and allows them to grasp a deeper understanding for STEM concepts.
