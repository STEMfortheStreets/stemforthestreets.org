STEM for the Streets

A student-led nonprofit organization based in Washington, D.C. aimed at increasing minority participation in STEM

DONATE

STEM for the Streets connects underrepresented minority youth with scientists, engineers, and doctors and teaches them STEM through hands-on labs and engaging lesson plans. Please donate so we can continue our mission of increasing minority participation within STEM.
