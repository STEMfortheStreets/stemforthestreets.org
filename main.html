START A CHAPTER
If you wish to start a STEM for the Streets chapter in your area please fill out the form found on the bottom of this page so our team can interview you and explain the procedures for starting a chapter.
CONTACT US
If you have any questions regarding starting a chapter of STEM for the Streets, please feel free to contact us via our email stem4thestreets@gmail.com  or through the contact form of our website.

APPLY TO START A CHAPTER

If you are interested in starting a chapter of STEM for the Streets please fill out the information below and our team will contact you as soon as possible in order to get you started. This form is for high school and college students who wish to start a chapter of STEM for the Streets to serve a middle school in their area. If you do not receive an email within a few days of turning in this form, please send us a follow up email stem4thestreets@gmail.com
Once you have applied, we will send you an email in the following days in order to schedule an online interview. Afterwards, if you are qualified to start a chapter, we will email you lesson plans, mail you lab materials, connect you with possible guest speakers, and assist you in beginning a chapter at a middle school in your area.
